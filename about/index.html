About Us

Your Connected Hardware Partner

We make it easy for developers to build connected devices and get to production quickly with innovative solutions.

Our Mission

To empower developers and makers worldwide with high-quality, easy-to-use development tools and hardware platforms.

What We Do

We design and manufacture development kits, modules, and accessories for building IoT and embedded systems. Our products are used by:

  • Hobbyists and makers
  • Professional developers
  • Educational institutions
  • Research organizations
  • Commercial product teams

Our Values

  • Quality: We build products that we’d want to use ourselves
  • Innovation: We stay at the forefront of technology
  • Community: We support and grow with our users
  • Open Source: We believe in sharing knowledge and code
  • Support: We’re here to help you succeed
